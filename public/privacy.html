1. Introduction

Tabi Travel Planner ("we", "our", "us") provides a travel planning service through a Progressive Web App (PWA) and an integrated AI-powered assistant.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

2.1 Email Address

  • Used for secure authentication via magic link login.
  • Required to access and manage your saved trips.

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Trip itineraries
  • Travel preferences
  • Notes and planning details entered by users

We do not collect:

  • Payment information
  • Device fingerprinting data
  • Location tracking data
  • Behavioral analytics data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Provide access to saved trips
  • Store and retrieve itinerary data
  • Maintain and improve service functionality

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

4. AI Integration

Tabi Travel Planner integrates with an AI assistant via API to help generate travel itineraries and recommendations.

When you use AI features:

  • Trip-related content may be processed to generate responses.
  • The AI system does not independently create user accounts.
  • No additional personal data beyond what you provide is collected directly by the GPT interface.

5. Data Storage and Hosting

  • Data is stored using industry-standard security practices.
  • We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information.
